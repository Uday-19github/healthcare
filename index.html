What does G-1 Health Care do?

G-1 Health Care is a leading surgical healthcare provider offering advanced elective surgical treatments through a network of specialist surgeons and modern hospitals across India.

What services do you offer?

We specialise in minimally invasive surgical procedures across proctology, urology, ENT, gynaecology, vascular diseases, orthopaedics, ophthalmology, fertility, weight-loss and aesthetics.

How do I book an appointment?

Use the booking form on this page, call our 24×7 helpline, or click "Book Free Appointment" anywhere on the site. A care coordinator will reach out within minutes.

Where are your hospitals located?

We have hospitals and partner clinics in 30+ Indian cities including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, Kolkata and several Tier-2 cities like Indore, Jaipur, Lucknow, Coimbatore and more.

Are treatments covered by insurance?

Yes — most procedures are covered by major insurance providers. Our team handles end-to-end paperwork so you focus on recovery, not bills.

Do you offer post-surgery care?

Absolutely. Every patient receives follow-up consultations, recovery instructions, dietary guidance and exercise plans for a smooth return to daily life.

Can I get a second opinion?

Yes. We offer second-opinion consultations from our specialists so you can make an informed decision about your treatment.

What makes G-1 Health Care different?

A patient-centric approach, advanced minimally invasive techniques, seamless insurance support and dedicated post-operative care — all under one roof.
